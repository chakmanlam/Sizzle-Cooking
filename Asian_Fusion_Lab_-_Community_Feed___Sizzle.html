üî• Spicy Miso Ramen

Prep Time
30 min
Cook Time
12 hrs
Servings
4
Difficulty
Hard

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork bones
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup red miso paste
  • 3 tbsp chili oil
  • 4 portions fresh ramen noodles
  • 4 soft-boiled eggs
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • 4 sheets nori seaweed
  • Green onions, sliced

Instructions

  1. Roast pork bones at 400¬∞F for 30 minutes until golden brown
  2. Simmer bones in water for 12 hours to create rich tonkotsu broth
  3. Strain broth and mix with chicken stock, miso paste, and chili oil
  4. Cook ramen noodles according to package directions
  5. Assemble bowls with noodles, broth, soft-boiled eggs, bean sprouts, nori, and green onions

üåÆ Korean BBQ Tacos

Prep Time
20 min
Cook Time
15 min
Servings
6
Difficulty
Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef short ribs, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 12 small corn tortillas
  • 2 cups kimchi, chopped
  • 1 cup cilantro
  • Lime wedges
  • Gochujang mayo

Instructions

  1. Marinate beef in soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, and sesame oil for 2 hours
  2. Grill beef over high heat for 2-3 minutes per side
  3. Warm tortillas on the grill
  4. Assemble tacos with beef, kimchi, cilantro, and gochujang mayo
  5. Serve with lime wedges

üåø Thai Basil Fried Rice

Prep Time
10 min
Cook Time
15 min
Servings
4
Difficulty
Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cooked jasmine rice (day-old works best)
  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 Thai chilies, sliced
  • 2 cups Thai basil leaves
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • Vegetable oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Heat wok over high heat with oil, scramble eggs and set aside
  2. Stir-fry chicken until cooked through, about 5 minutes
  3. Add garlic and chilies, cook for 30 seconds until fragrant
  4. Add rice and break up any clumps, stir-fry for 3 minutes
  5. Add fish sauce, soy sauce, and oyster sauce, mix well
  6. Toss in Thai basil and scrambled eggs, cook for 1 minute
  7. Serve immediately with lime wedges
